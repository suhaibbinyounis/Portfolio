About Me

Hello.

As a computer programmer for several years, I’ve spent a lot of time presenting interesting stories through ideas and creative thinking. I keep myself occupied with numerous initiatives that I find useful.

I’m currently working as a software developer at Bank of America, where I develop and maintain web applications and software solutions for various business needs. I have a B.Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering from SRM Institute of Science and Technology.

I’m also a self-proclaimed scientist who likes to explore various domains of computer science, such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, natural language processing, data mining, web development, and more. I have published a research paper on “Opinion mining on web-based communities using optimised clustering algorithms” in the Turkish Journal of Computer and Mathematics Education (TURCOMAT), which has been cited by 6+ other papers.

I enjoy writing blogs about various topics that fascinate me, such as reality, friendship, magic, and thoughts. You can check out my blog at suhaib.in, where I share my thoughts and opinions on various issues and ideas. Some of my blogs posted are:

  • What Is Reality?: A philosophical exploration of the nature of reality and how we perceive it.
  • Friendships Endure Over Distance and Time: A personal reflection on how friendships can last despite physical separation and time differences.
  • Dark Clouds: A poetic expression of the feelings of sadness and loneliness that sometimes overwhelm us.
  • Magic Behind Magic: A scientific analysis of the phenomenon of magic and how it works in the real world.
  • Thoughts 101: A collection of random thoughts and musings that I have from time to time.

I believe that once we die we are not coming back. So I try to do all the stuff that I ever wanted to do. It doesn’t really matter if I’m losing out on something I don’t care about. Life is a do-it-yourself project and it takes half your life to discover the fact.

Please don’t hesitate to get in touch with me if you share my interests and would like to collaborate.

Thanks & Regards,
